Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pay2Pay

How secure is Pay2Pay?
Pay2Pay uses bank-level security with 256-bit encryption and multi-factor authentication. We also implement biometric login (fingerprint and face ID) for added security. Your data is never shared with third parties.
Are there any fees for transactions?
Domestic transfers are completely free. For international transfers, we charge a small 1% fee (minimum $1). Currency conversion fees are 0.5% above the mid-market rate.
How long do transfers take?
Transfers between Pay2Pay users are instant. Bank transfers typically take 1-2 business days. International transfers may take 3-5 business days depending on the destination country.
Can I use Pay2Pay for business?
Yes! We offer special business accounts with features like invoicing, payroll management, and team access controls. Business accounts have different fee structures and higher transaction limits.
Which countries do you support?
Currently, we support 45 countries including USA, Canada, UK, Australia, and most European countries. We're expanding rapidly and plan to support 100+ countries by next year.

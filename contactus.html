Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team via phone at +91 123-4567, email at support@pay2pay.com, or through the contact form on this page. Our support hours are 9 AM to 9 PM, 7 days a week.
What are your office hours?
Our corporate office is open Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM. However, our customer support team is available 7 days a week from 9 AM to 9 PM to assist you with any inquiries or issues.
Do you offer business partnership opportunities?
Yes, we welcome business partnerships. Please contact our business development team at partnerships@pay2pay.com or fill out the contact form selecting "Business Inquiry" as the subject.
How long does it take to get a response?
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our customer support number for immediate assistance during business hours.
Can I visit your office without an appointment?
While we welcome visitors, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure the appropriate team member is available to assist you. You can schedule an appointment by calling our office number.
